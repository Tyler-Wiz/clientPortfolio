Toke Makinwa, J-Martins and Mike Aremu let out their playful side at the LG G5 launch

You know what they say; work hard and play harder. LG is ready to get you to play more with it's latest state of the art smartphone, the LG G5.The sophisticated smartphone which has won over 33 awards at Mobile World Conference 2016 was launched at an exclusive event on Friday 13th May, 2016.

Loaded with great features geared to make you stand out and have more fun, the LG G5 is a must have smartphone. It's impressive specifications, metal 16 MP wide angled camera and a unique slide-out expansion slot that can be used with a range of accessories promises to add a dash of fun into your life.

"At LG, we believe that 'Life's good when you play more' and we actually have enjoyed every step of creating the G5, dreaming to re-ignite your instinct to play," said Mr S.H Kim, MD LGEAF during his opening speech. "We think the smartphone's best days are still ahead. The LG G5 was created to unleash people's desire to play. It captures the excitement of what a smartphone is meant to be."

The launch event was attended by Toke Makinwa , J-Martins and Yemi Sax who had a great time exploring the cool, playful features of the LG G5. It was indeed a night to remember.So be prepared to #playmorenaija. The LG G5 can be purchased from Founani, Pointek and Slot outlets across the country. Life is indeed good when you play more.
